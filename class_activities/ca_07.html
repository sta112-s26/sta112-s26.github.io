Spurious correlations

The Spurious Correlations website contains many different examples of variables which are correlated, but for which no real relationship exists (hence the term spurious in the name).

The website also allows you to discover your own spurious correlations.

Experiment with finding different spurious correlations of your own. See how high you can make the correlation coefficient \(r\)!

And remember: it is highly unlikely that any of the correlations you see are real that is, there is no reason to expect any of these variables to be directly related, and any correlations observed are likely due to chance.
